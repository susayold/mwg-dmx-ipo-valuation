01 / The decision

Do not value MWG as one undifferentiated retailer.

The IPO provides a visible post-money anchor for DMX. The analytical work is separating the remaining MWG assets, parent adjustments and corporate perimeter without double-counting EraBlue or IPO cash.

Core question
After valuing MWG's post-IPO stake in DMX, what value belongs to Bach Hoa Xanh, An Khang, AvaKids and parent-level adjustments?
MWG equity=DMX stake+Non-DMX stub±Parent adjustments
